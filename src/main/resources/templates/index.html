About Us

EduSync is an Academic Management System designed to help educational institutions streamline administrative tasks efficiently. It enables schools and colleges to manage faculty profiles, class schedules, course assignments, Faculty availability, leave management, and performance evaluations all in one place. By automating routine processes, EduSync enhances faculty and student interaction, reduces manual workload, and ensures a smooth academic workflow.
